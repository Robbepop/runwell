[−][src]Module runwell_ir::primitive

Runwell primitive types and constant values.

Unlike Wasm the Runwell JIT supports 8-bit and 16-bit integer types. This is to generalize certain compound Wasm operations such as i32.load8_u that first load an 8-bit integer from the given address and then zero-extends it to a 32-bit integer value.
